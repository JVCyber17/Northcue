    Your privacy.

    The documents people bring to Northcue can feel personal. Here is how we look after them, simply.

    1. You upload
    3. Northcue structures it
    5. AI helps phrase it
    7. It is deleted

    Your document is deleted after we read it. We keep only anonymous information, never its contents.

    The thinking is ours

    Northcue is not a chatbot. We built our own system that reads your document, works out what it appears to be, and carefully decides what belongs on each cue card and how cautiously to word it. That structure, and the safety behind it, is Northcue's own work. An AI provider only helps put it into clear, friendly language at the end.

    What we keep

    Only anonymous information, like the type of document and whether it read clearly. Not linked to you.

    What we never keep

    Your document, its text, or your name, address, and account numbers.

    How your cards are made

    Northcue structures your cue cards and decides what matters. To make them, an AI provider reads your document text, including any personal details in it, and helps phrase the cards clearly. That text is only used to make your cards. It is processed securely, it is not used to train AI, and it is not stored.
